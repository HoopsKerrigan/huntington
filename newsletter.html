Newsletter

For those of you who can't get enough of the Huntington Website, send us an electronic-mail request to huntingtonwvcity@gmail.com to enroll in a one-page weekly newsletter with other happenings around the Greater Huntington Area. Enrollment is free and processing your account should take no more than 24 business hours. In addition to reminders of local events and smaller news stories, the Huntington Newsletter is printable and includes advertisements, coupons, and interactive games.

Newsletters are mailed every Sunday for your enjoyment!
