About

The Huntington Website is curated by a special group of town employees who work tirelessly to ensure the website runs smoothly on all computers that access it. This website is a symbol of great effort by workers in the City of Huntington Mayor's Office, the Cabel County Public Library System, and the City of Huntington Technology Office.

For those looking to submit a report or issue with the website, please contact the City of Huntington Technology Office with your concern.

Gourd Fact of the Day

The name 'turkey', shortened from 'turkey hen', was originally applied to guinea fowl which were imported through Turkish merchants. This name was incorrectly given to the wild bird native to North America when they began to enter the world market.
